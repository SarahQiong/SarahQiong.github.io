About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate under the supervision of Professor Jiahua Chen. My main research interest centres on combining the Gaussian mixture models with the optimal transportation to solve various machine learning problems, such as distributed clustering and approximate Bayesian inference.

I received my M.Sc. degree in Statistics (2017) from University of British Columbia and my B.Sc. degree in Statistics (2015) from the School of the Gifted Young (SCGY) at the University of Science and Technology of China.
